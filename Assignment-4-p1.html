The Kitties to Go Story

We originally started in Long Island, unfortunately people with allergy threatened us with law suites, the cats wouldn't stop talking about the New England Patriots, laying on car horns or scratching people for bumping into them on the sidewalks. A distant traveller one day told us about a place called Seattle, a land where you can do what you want as long as you don't use your car horn, complain about people running late or become bothered by a confusing zoning system and have the anarchist May Day Parade where anarchist show up at a permitted time and follow a route laid out by Law Enforcement. We quickly moved to Seattle and found a home where nothing making sense was normal
